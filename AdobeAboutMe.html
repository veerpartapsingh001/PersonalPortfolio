About Me

Year:2021

Description

This year I started my Web development career, with the help of the "About Me" website project, given to me and my peers to start us off with our education, family and friends, future "dream" travel, colleges of interest, plans, and contact information in mind and on the page.
Since we were still new to the concepts of programming we used the guidelines that came with the template but were still lenient enough to encourage us to build the site from scratch and be more creative. Adding our personal touches came with adding profile pictures and talking about them in a detailed manner. I also learned the importance of giving credit where it's due. For example I learned to give credit to the artist when using their work.

Skills/Software used

  • Adobe Spark

Problems

I came across some difficulties when working on this project, one of which was organizing the text and images in a way that complements each other as just placing an obnoxiously large image with no constants affects the text formatting and makes it hard for the user to understand the image.

How I Solved Them

Working on this project I realized that a key component was Communicate and working on the share/exchange of information, news, or ideas was something I was able to work on as it is one of the Habits of Mind of CART. Which is something I hope to use in the future.
